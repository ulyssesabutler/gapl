# Parsing

## Lexing

### Keywords
- `function`
- `interface`
- `sequential`
- `combinational`
- `parameter`

### Punctuation
- `( )` PARANTHESES
- `{ }` CURLY BRACES
- `[ ]` SQUARE BRACKET
- `< >` ANGLE BRACKET
- `:` COLON
- `;` SEMI COLON
- `EOF` EOF

### Operators
- `=>` CONNECTOR
- `.` PERIOD
- `,` COMMA
- `*` MULTIPLY
- `+` ADD
- `-` SUBTRACT
- `==` EQUAL
- `!=` NOT_EQUAL
- `<` LESS_THAN
- `>` GREATER_THAN
- `<=` LESS_THAN_EQUAL
- `>=` GREATER_THAN_EQUAL
